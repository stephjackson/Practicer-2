Practicer

Practicer is a utility to help musicians focus their practice and improve faster. It can be used to create practice schedules, and individual exercises to fill those schedules with. During practice, you can use it to keep track of how cleanly you're playing, and Practicer will automatically keep track of how you're improving. Practice doesn't make perfect - perfect practice does, and Practicer is the web app to help get you there.
